🌾 AgriGuardian

An edge-first, multi-device precision agriculture system designed to protect crops and optimize irrigation. Driven by local physical sensing, Qualcomm NPU acceleration (LiteRT + QNN), and macro-geospatial Earth Observation feeds (ISRO VEDAS).

Qualcomm Edge AI

Runs MobileNetV2 leaf disease classifiers locally on the NPU via the LiteRT runtime, ensuring 100% offline, zero-latency diagnostics directly on-device.

ISRO VEDAS Data Fusion

Queries regional Earth Observation grids to fetch live NDVI index, land surface temperature, and soil wetness profiles, matching micro-sensors to prevent over-irrigation.

Multi-Device Orchestration

Bridges real-time sensor streams from the STM32U585 MCU to the Dragonwing Linux MPU, syncing state controls instantly to the phone client over WebSockets.

System Interconnection Layout
